About

Abstract:

Social justice factors are at the heart of the ongoing modern civil rights movement. Food justice is an extension of these struggles, where food insecurity, malnutrition, and lack of access disproportionately affect social classes otherwise suffering from parallel disadvantages. 48 million Americans are lacking consistent access to nutritionally sufficient foods, roughly half of whom live in food deserts devoid of fresh grocery options. Our map aims to bring attention to these disparities.
